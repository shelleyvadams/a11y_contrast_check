Rating details & definitions
Great!
The contrast ratio is 7:1 or higher. This follows the WCAG 2.0 Level AAA Contrast (Enhanced) guideline.
Good.
The contrast ratio is 4.5:1 or higher. This follows:
  • The WCAG 2.0 Level AA Contrast (Minimum) guideline, and
  • The WCAG 2.0 Level AAA Contrast (Enhanced) guideline for large scale text.
Acceptable for large text.
The contrast ratio is 3:1 or higher. This follows the WCAG 2.0 Level AA Contrast (Minimum) guideline for large scale text.
Poor.
The contrast ratio is less than 3:1. Unless this is Incidental text or text within a logo, it does not follow with the WCAG 2.0 contrast guidelines. Depending on other factors, it may still follow the WCAG 2.0 Level A Use of Color guideline.
Large scale text
Defined as …at least 18 point or 14 point bold… roughly equivalent to 1.2 and 1.5 em…; given typical defaults:
  • 1.2em = 19.2px,
  • 1.5em = 24px.
